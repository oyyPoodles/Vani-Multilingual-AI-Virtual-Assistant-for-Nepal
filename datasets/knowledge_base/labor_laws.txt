Nepal Labor Laws — Summary for VANI Knowledge Base

1. Working Hours
- Standard: 8 hours per day, 48 hours per week.
- Overtime: Maximum 4 hours per day, paid at 1.5x regular rate.
- Night shift (10 PM - 6 AM): Additional allowance required.

2. Minimum Wage (2024-2025)
- Monthly: NPR 17,300 (basic) + NPR 2,000 (dearness allowance) = NPR 19,300 total.
- Daily wage: NPR 627.
- Adjusted annually by the Minimum Wage Fixation Committee.

3. Leave Entitlements
- Annual leave: 18 days per year (1.5 days per month).
- Sick leave: 12 days per year (half-pay after 1 year).
- Public holidays: 13 gazetted holidays per year.
- Maternity leave: 98 days (60 days paid).
- Paternity leave: 15 days.
- Mourning leave: 13 days.

4. Social Security Fund (SSF)
- Employee contribution: 11% of basic salary.
- Employer contribution: 20% of basic salary.
- Covers: Medical, accident, disability, old-age, dependent family.

5. Termination and Severance
- Notice period: 30 days for permanent employees.
- Severance pay: 1 month's salary per year of service.
- Retrenchment requires IRD notification.

6. Provident Fund
- Both employer and employee contribute 10% of basic salary.
- Managed by Employee Provident Fund Organization.

7. Gratuity
- Applicable after 3 years of continuous service.
- Rate: Half month's salary per year of service.

8. Important Contacts
- Department of Labor: www.dol.gov.np
- Social Security Fund: www.ssf.gov.np
- Labor Court for disputes

9. Child Labor
- Minimum working age: 16 years.
- 14-16: Only light, non-hazardous work, max 6 hours/day.
- Under 14: Strictly prohibited.

10. Contract Requirements
- Written employment contracts mandatory for all workers.
- Must specify: position, salary, working hours, leave, termination terms.

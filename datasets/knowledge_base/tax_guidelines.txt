Nepal Tax Guidelines — Summary for VANI Knowledge Base

1. Income Tax Rates (Individuals) — FY 2081/82 (2024/25)
- Up to NPR 5,00,000: 1%
- NPR 5,00,001 - 7,00,000: 10%
- NPR 7,00,001 - 10,00,000: 20%
- NPR 10,00,001 - 20,00,000: 30%
- Above NPR 20,00,000: 36%

2. Corporate Tax Rates
- General businesses: 25%
- Banks and financial institutions: 30%
- Manufacturing industries: 20%
- Special industries (export): 15%

3. Tax Filing Deadlines
- Individual tax return: Within 3 months of fiscal year end (Ashoj end / mid-October).
- Corporate tax return: Within 3 months of fiscal year end.
- Nepal fiscal year: Shrawan 1 to Ashad 31 (mid-July to mid-July).

4. Withholding Tax (TDS)
- Salary: As per income tax slab
- Rent: 10%
- Interest: 5% (for residents), 15% (for non-residents)
- Dividends: 5%
- Consultancy/Service fees: 15%

5. Tax Deductions Allowed
- Life insurance premium (up to NPR 40,000)
- Medical insurance (up to NPR 20,000)
- Provident fund contributions
- Donation to approved organizations (up to 5% of taxable income)
- Home loan interest (up to NPR 1,50,000 for first home)

6. PAN Registration
- Permanent Account Number required for all taxpayers.
- Apply at local IRD office or online at www.ird.gov.np.
- Required documents: Citizenship, photos, business registration (if applicable).

7. Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Late filing: 0.1% per month
- Non-filing: Tax assessment by IRD + penalties
- Tax evasion: Criminal prosecution possible

8. Important Contacts
- Inland Revenue Department: www.ird.gov.np
- Tax Helpline: 1660-01-60060
- E-filing portal: taxpayerportal.ird.gov.np
